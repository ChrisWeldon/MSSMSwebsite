Student Senate


The Maine School Of Science and Mathematics Student Senate is dedicated to making MSSM student lives happier and healthier. The Senate works to provide students with administrative representaion, enjoyable residence, and activities throughout the year.


This site can be used to checkup on Senate Minutes, Look for upcoming activities, and connect with senate though suggestion or contact.
